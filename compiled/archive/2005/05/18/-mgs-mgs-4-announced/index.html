[MGS]MGS 4 announced!

On E3 Expo, Hideo Kojima announced new MGS title. Which will be MGS 4. On the official Metal Gear Solid Site, a new link pointing to "Kojima Productions" appeared, which mentioned "MGS 4", as well as a power-up version of MGS 3 (I know it would come) and Metal Gear Acid 2. The promotion movie of new MGS also comes out at this address: mms://wm1.streaming.ne.jp/konami2/kojimapro_e32k5_e_n4v8.asf In my last blog about new MGS, I mentioned that in new MGS there will be "no place for Hideo Kojima", well, forget about that. In the promo, Hideo changed it to "No Replacement for Hideo Kojima". Good news? At least for MGS 4. In the promo we can see Raiden and Snake fighting to seize the place for the "main character", and of course Snake won, but maybe Raiden (or someone looks like him) will also appear in the new MGS. I guess CQC would still play important role in new MGS, and there could be more drastic environment changing, which makes you "no place to hide". Hideo also played parody on Splinter Cell and Sam Fisher. He knows quite well about his enemy: XBOX 360 and UBI, huh?
