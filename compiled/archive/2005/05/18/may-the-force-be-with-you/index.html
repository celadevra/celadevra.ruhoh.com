May the Force be with you

It's May 19th, 09:02 AM, a few hours before the time when Star Wars Episode 3: the Revenge of the Sith hit theatres and cinemas all over the world.

This can be a very sentimental moment. When a legend which lasts over 30 years finally comes to an end, we may be a bit lost. But before I see this movie, there is more anticipitation than sadness in me.

Most critics speak highly of this closing episode to the Star Wars Epic. Some lucky boy who saw it last night say it's kinda na\"ive. No matter what they say, I want to see it myself and have my own private feeling about it.
